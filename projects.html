How We Tackled Each Project

Modern Patio Oasis

Materials: 900 × 600 mm porcelain slabs, cedar, LED uplights.

Challenges: Poor drainage solved with recessed ACO channels.

Highlights: Seamless indoor-outdoor threshold & custom bench.

Front Drive Revival

Materials: Clay block pavers, granite sett edging, turf.

Challenges: Removed 40 m2 of cracked concrete without damaging utilities.

Highlights: Permeable base meets SUDS regs; fresh planting scheme.

Tiered Sleeper Garden

Materials: Treated sleepers, decorative gravel, native shrubs.

Challenges: 1 m height difference required safe terracing.

Highlights: Integrated steps & hidden irrigation for easy upkeep.
